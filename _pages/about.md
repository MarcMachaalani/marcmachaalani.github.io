I am a PGY-1 resident at the [Yale Traditional Internal Medicine Residency](https://medicine.yale.edu/internal-medicine/education/residency/). I received my medical degree from the [Lebanese University](http://medicine.ul.edu.lb/) in 2023, after which I completed my postdoctoral research fellowship in genitourinary oncology at [Dana-Farber Cancer Institute](https://labs.dana-farber.org/choueirilab/people/marc-machaalani-md/) and [Harvard Medical School](https://connects.catalyst.harvard.edu/Profiles/display/Person/217637/) under the mentorship of [Dr. Toni K. Choueiri](https://labs.dana-farber.org/choueirilab/). My research has focused on integrative analyses of circulating and tumor biomarkers in renal cell carcinoma, with particular interests in tumor–host interactions, immune evasion, the gut microbiome, and cancer genomics.

My work has been [published](/publications/) in leading journals including *Nature Medicine*, *Nature Communications*, and the *Journal of Clinical Oncology*, and has been [presented](/talks/) at international meetings including the American Society of Clinical Oncology annual meeting (ASCO) and genitourinary symposium (ASCO GU), the European Society of Medical Oncology (ESMO) annual congress, and the Society for Immunotherapy of Cancer (SITC) annual meeting. I have been the recipient of several national and international [honors](/awards/), including multiple ASCO Conquer Cancer and ESMO Merit Awards.
