Marc received his M.D. from the Lebanese University, Lebanon, in 2023. He is currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute, where he works under the mentorship of [Dr. Toni K. Choueiri](https://www.labs.dana-farber.org/choueirilab/). His research focuses on the integrative analyses of circulating and tumor biomarkers, and the impact of host immunity, gut dysbiosis, and tumor genomics on clinical outcomes. Outside of medicine, Marc is an accomplished pianist and enjoys reading, biking, and playing tennis.
