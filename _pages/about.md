Marc received his M.D. from the Lebanese University, Lebanon, in 2023. He is currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute, where he works under the mentorship of [Dr. Toni K. Choueiri](https://labs.dana-farber.org/choueirilab/). His research focuses on the integrative analyses of circulating and tumor biomarkers, and the impact of host immunity, gut dysbiosis, and tumor genomics on clinical outcomes. 

He has contributed to multiple high-impact publications across leading journals including Nature Medicine, Nature Communications, Journal of Clinical Oncology, JAMA Network Open, European Urology, and ESMO Open, with research spanning kidney cancer metabolism, immune evasion, tumor microenvironment biology, circulating biomarkers, and health disparities. He has presented his work at major international meetings including ASCO, ASCO GU, ESMO, SITC, and the Kidney Cancer Research Summit, through both oral and poster presentations. Marc is the recipient of several national and international honors, including multiple ASCO Conquer Cancer and ESMO Merit Awards. His research has been supported by competitive fellowships and travel grants.

Outside of medicine, Marc is an accomplished classical pianist and enjoys reading, biking, and playing tennis.
